GYM AND SPA

At KK GYM we offer a friendly and inviting atmosphere with excellent training experience, that meets the health and fitness needs of all members. The gym offers a free weights area with a wide range of dumbbells, barbells, plates, benches, stretching and floor exercises area, cardio and strength training equipment. We offer Pilates classes at our premises. In addition to it we have an amazing rugby field where members can run out doors. Whether your goal is general fitness, weight loss, toning, core and strength training, functional fitness, or sports specific training, we have qualified trainers who specialise in all areas of fitness to devise a customised training plan, motivate and guide you to achieve your goals in a most enjoyable way.
